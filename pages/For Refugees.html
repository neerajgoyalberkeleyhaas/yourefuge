For Refugees

YouRefuge enables you to get cash immediately for your valuables, instead of leaving them behind to be looted.

How it Works

Share your story, post your item for sale, and finally select if you want the option of buying your item back within 1, 2 or 3 years.

Once your item has been selected for purchase, bring your item to a designated shelter and go through verification (using government issued ID and facial recognition technology). You will have the option of receiving your funds in a prepaid card, or a digital wallet.

If you selected the buy-back option, YouRefuge will hold the item safely for up to three years. If you do not need the buyback option, YouRefuge will deliver the item to the buyer on your behalf.
